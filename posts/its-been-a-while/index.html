It's Been a While

I’ve left this site abandoned for quite some time, but now I am in need of a place to host a small choose-your-own-adventure game I am making, so I will be using this site here again. I might post stuff about other projects I am working on at the moment too every now and then, if only to keep track of everything myself. They say that writing things down helps organize one’s thoughts. Whoever they are.

I might switch from using Hugo to Jekyll before I release the game to the public, mainly because I am encountering a lot of problems when building the site. This changes nothing for you, just means a bit more pain in the ass for me temporarily, in order to avoid future pains in the ass.

Anyway, see ya!
