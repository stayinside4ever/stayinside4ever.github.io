Drawabox Lesson 1

I started Drawabox lessons back in autumn. I never had the patience or willpower to stick through all of them, so I slowly started doing less and less after finishing Lesson 1. Never put that up for critique, either, because I was scared of criticism, since I’m quite harsh on myself as it is. But now I want to start doing the lessons again from scratch, so I’m putting what I have done up for critique, to know where I should improve this time around.

Oh, also, one page of the sketchbook (the more yellow-ish paper) is about the size of an A3 paper sheet, only more square in shape. The white-ish pages are A4. This is more of a note for future me who will have forgotten which ones are which and that I even had a big sketchbook.

So, first off, superimposed lines. I’m actually kinda proud of the straight ones, since I have shaky hands.

Next up, ghosting. Only one page, as was said in the homework.

After that, planes exercise. Yes, I got lazy when I got to the second page.

Ellipse tables! These were actually quite fun to draw, haha.

Funnels.

The 2 point perspective exercise! The white pages are starting to appear, since I did most of these away from home.

1 point perspective.

Rotating boxes. My nightmare. Don’t ask why I tried doing the huge thing, I don’t know either.

And finally, the organic perspective exercise. Man, this was hell, too.

Aaaand that’s it! Hope to hear your thoughts in the comments of my reddit post!
